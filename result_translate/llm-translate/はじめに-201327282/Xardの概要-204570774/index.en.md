**Xard (エクサード)** is a next-generation card issuing platform that allows you to issue your own original Visa card or JCB card easily and at low cost.

With various issuance forms, it responds to the business needs of various companies, regardless of type, such as Fintech companies, financial institutions, retail businesses, and web service providers.

By incorporating the various API functions provided by Xard into your own services, you can create new experiences. Infcurion designs businesses with customers based on extensive knowledge in the payment and financial fields.
