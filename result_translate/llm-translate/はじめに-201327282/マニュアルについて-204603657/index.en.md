## 3\. How to Read the Manual

The icons and terms used in this manual are as follows:

### Icons Used in the Manual

**Icon**

**Content**

Things that must be done or noteworthy points are noted.

Convenient information or supplementary information is noted.

### Terms Used in the Manual

**Term**

**Content**

Acquirer

A card company that acquires and manages international brand merchants.

Printing Company

A company that prints and sends mail such as Physical cards based on Xard's requests.

Infcurion

The operating company of Xard.  
It performs processing related to card issuance operations such as international brand card issuance and settlement.

International Brand

The operating company of an international brand network.

International Brand Merchant

An international brand merchant where cards issued by Xard can be used.

International Brand Member (BIN Sponsor)

A company that provides the necessary BIN (Bank Identification Number) or IIN (Issuer Identification Number) for international brand card issuance.

Partner

A partner company that receives Xard's service provision.  
Partners acquire users and manage members.

User

Card users acquired by partners (corporations, sole proprietors, or individuals).

Real-time Switching

A method of payment processing where balance management is conducted by the partner. A partner's unique Funding Source is used in real-time at the time of settlement.
