This manual explains how to install Xard for partners who will be building an Xard environment and making API connections.

## 3\. How to read the manual

The meanings of the icons and terms used in this manual are as follows.

### Icons used in the manual

**Icon**

**Contents**

It describes things that must be done and things that should be especially noted.

Contains useful information and supplementary information.

### Terms used in the manual

**Term**

**Contents**

acquirer

A credit card company that acquires and manages merchants affiliated with international brands.

printing company

This is a company that prints and ships physical cards and other mailings in response to Xard's requests.

Infucurion

This is the operating company of Xard.
We perform processing related to card issuance operations such as issuance of international brand cards and payments.

international brands

A company that operates an international brand network.

International brand member stores

This is an international brand affiliated store that accepts cards issued by Xard.

International Brand Member (BIN Sponsor)

We are an international brand member company that provides the BIN (Bank Identification Number) or IIN (Issuer Identification Number) required to issue international brand cards.

partner

This is a partner company that receives services from Xard.
Partners acquire users and manage membership.

user

Card users (corporations, sole proprietors, or individuals) acquired by partners.

real-time switching

This is a payment processing method where the partner manages the balance.Use partner-specific Funding Sources in real-time at the time of payment.
