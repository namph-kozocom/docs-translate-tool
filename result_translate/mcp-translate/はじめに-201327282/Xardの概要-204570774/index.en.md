Xard is a next-generation card issuance platform that allows you to issue your own original Visa or JCB cards easily and at low cost.

With various issuance formats, it supports a wide range of business needs, including Fintech companies, financial institutions, retailers, and web service providers, regardless of their form.

By integrating the various API functions provided by Xard into your own services, you can create new experiences. Infcurion, with its wealth of knowledge in the payment and financial fields, designs businesses together with its customers.
